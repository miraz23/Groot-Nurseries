Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in the plant industry, our journey has been rooted in passion and dedication.

How It All Started

Groot Nurseries began as a small family-owned garden center in 2020. What started as a passion project with just a few plant varieties has grown into one of the region's most trusted sources for quality plants, gardening supplies, and expert advice.

Over the years, we've expanded our operations while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer service. Today, we serve thousands of customers across the country, helping them create beautiful green spaces in their homes and gardens.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our plants, shipping, and care instructions.

We take great care in packaging our plants, but occasionally damage may occur during transit. If your plant arrives damaged, please take photos of the packaging and plant within 24 hours of delivery and contact our customer service team. We'll promptly arrange for a replacement or refund.

Many common houseplants can be toxic to pets if ingested. We clearly mark pet-friendly plants in our product descriptions. If you have pets, we recommend researching plants before purchasing or selecting from our "Pet-Friendly" collection. Popular pet-safe options include spider plants, Boston ferns, and calatheas.

Due to the perishable nature of plants, we don't accept returns. However, we stand behind the quality of our plants with a 30-day guarantee. If your plant shows signs of distress within 30 days of delivery despite following proper care instructions, we'll replace it or provide a store credit.
